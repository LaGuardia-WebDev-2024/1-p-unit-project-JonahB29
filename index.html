Gymnastics, A Crash Course!

There are many different sports of Gymnastics. For the purpouses of this webpage we will focus on Artistic Gymnastics. There are 2 types of Artics Gymnastics, Mens and Womens.

Mens Gymnastics

Mens Gymnastics has 6 events:

  1. Floor Exercise
  2. Pommel Horse
  3. Still Rings
  4. Vault
  5. Parallel Bars
  6. High Bar

Womens Gymnastics

Womens Gymnastics has 4 events:

  1. Vault
  2. Uneaven Bars
  3. Balance Beam
  4. Floor Exercise
